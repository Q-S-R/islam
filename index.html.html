What is Islam?

Islam is a religion of peace, mercy, and forgiveness. It is the faith revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) over 1400 years ago. Islam teaches the belief in one God (Allah), and following the teachings of the Qur'an and the Prophet's sunnah.

The Five Pillars of Islam

  • Shahada: The testimony of faith – "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger."
  • Salat: Performing the five daily prayers.
  • Zakat: Giving charity to the poor and needy.
  • Sawm: Fasting during the month of Ramadan.
  • Hajj: The pilgrimage to Mecca at least once in a lifetime if able.

The Message of Islam

Islam calls for worshipping Allah alone, showing kindness to others, and living a life of morality and purpose. It encourages knowledge, patience, and striving for good. Islam is a way of life that brings inner peace, community harmony, and connection with the Creator.
